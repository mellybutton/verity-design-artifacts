Feature title
Enterprise POC exception handling
Problem statement

Enterprise accounts in POC require conditional handling that the current product does not support — legal review triggers, milestone escalation paths, and executive looping are all applied manually by CS. This creates inconsistent outcomes and depends entirely on who is assigned to the account.

Acceptance criteria
System flags accounts in POC with contract value ≥ $50k and prompts CS to add legal to thread
Legal review tracked per SOW — escalation fires automatically at 5 business days
Missed POC milestones trigger executive check-in prompt within 48 hours
All escalation events logged to account timeline with CSM attribution
