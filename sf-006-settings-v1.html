Domains

Domains define how rules are grouped and how conflicts between rules in the same domain are resolved. Once rules of a domain type are active, the policy for that domain is locked.

◈
Policy locking is a governance guarantee, not a bug. It ensures that the reasoning behind your active rules stays consistent. If you need to change a policy, all rules of that domain type must be archived first.
